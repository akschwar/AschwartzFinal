WHO WE ARE

A LEGACY BAKED WITH LOVE

A Generational Business

Ms. Jackson is a successful business owner with a bake shop in downtown Lancaster. She wants to be able to send her child, Markus, to college after he graduates high school. Ms. Jackson grew up with her grandparents and had a close relationship with her grandmother. She learnt to bake from her grandmother as a child. After her grandparents passed away, she inherited her grandmother’s bake shop. She now wants to take it online so that she could grow her business.
